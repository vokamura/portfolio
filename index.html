About me

My name is Vikki, and I'm a web developer.

I discovered my passion for web development after I spent some time as a staff accountant, as I needed a challenge where I could be creative and strengthen my problem-solving capabilities. I graduated with a B.S. in Business Administration from Cal Poly-San Luis Obispo, with concentrations in Information Systems and Accounting.

When I'm not programming, I enjoy listening to live music, visiting museums, hanging out at the beach, volunteering for the Girl Scouts, and petting dogs.
