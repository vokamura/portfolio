About me

My name is Vikki, and I'm a front end web developer. I love being able to create with code and being hands-on with the projects I work on and enjoy collaborating in a team and being able to find creative ways to be innovative.

I attended an accelerated web development program so that I could transition careers from accounting to web/tech. I graduated from Cal Poly - San Luis Obispo, with a B.S. in Business Administration with concentrations in Information Systems and Accounting.

After spending time as a staff accountant, I felt the need for the ability to problem-solve and create. I decided to go into web development as I realized through code, I could be creative, and have a passion for it.

When I'm not programming, I enjoy going to concerts and museums, eating at new places, exploring, hanging out at the beach, volunteering for the Girl Scouts, and petting dogs.
